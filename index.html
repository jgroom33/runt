The Runt — Diaz Suspension Design dual-chamber air spring

Three forks under the same applied load — stock air spring (left), a coil fork (middle), and the Runt (right, teal). The coil is nearly linear but preloaded, so it starts off firm. The stock air spring is supple early then ramps hard. The Runt does nothing until the load passes its set pressure (~125 psi), then its second stage adds support — lifting the mid-stroke and keeping travel in reserve when the others dive.
